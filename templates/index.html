About-


SurfMate is a multi-purpose companion app that makes your beach experience much more fun! From checking if it's a good day to surf or not by giving you the weather or to seeing if it's going to be crowded   or not. We have got you covered! To forward the mission of cleaning the shores and making it a pleasant experience for everyone while caring for our planet Earth- This app has it's own Garbage Classifier - which categorises waste into Organic or Recyclable, for efficient waste management. We also introduce your very own personal assistant-  Alex! which gives you helpful resources such as contacts of Police, Ambulance, Nearby Lifeguards and even the fun stuff such as Nearby Restaurants!


This was supposed to be an Android App but due to time constraints and the lack of skillset we made our hack into a Web-App that can be used on all devices such as Phones, Desktop, Laptop, Tablets etc. We plan to work on this project further in future .   



  • Creating an Android App. The prototype can be viewed here
  • Making design changes for better user experience and overall aesthetics
  • Optimize the loading times of our Web-App
